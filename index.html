About Me

WHO AM I?

Hi I'm Katherine Nguyen but most people call me Kat. I'm an aspiring programmer and am currently enrolled in a web development program to further my career. I've studied art, design and some animation at San Jose State University and obtained a bachelor's degree in Design Studies. From there I became a freelance artist/designer. However, that didn't stop my hunger for more knowledge as I became interested in web development and computer programming. I want to do more than just illustrate. This is my future career. I aim to contribute my skills, work with other individuals and collaborate with companies in building extraordinary websites. I want to help create unique designs and astonishing visuals for others to see and interact with. My main goal is to help improve the user experience.
